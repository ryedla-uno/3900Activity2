Baxter Arena at UNO Part2



Volleyball Court

The 2022 Summit League Volleyball Championship will take place November 24-26 at Baxter Arena in Omaha, Neb. The top six teams at the conclusion of the regular season advance to the championship. The winner of the Summit League Championship receives the league's automatic bid to the NCAA Tournament.


Basketball Court

The 2023 Summit League Men's & Women's Basketball Championship takes place March 3-7 at the Denny Sanford PREMIER Center in Sioux Falls, S.D. The tournament champions will receive automatic berths to the NCAA Division I Basketball Championship.
